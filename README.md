A retrait from sinne: Wherein is sounded, as with a shrill trumpet in the eares of all men, what damnable daungers depend vpon continuance in sin, neglect of repentance, abuse of the acceptable time of grace: and other excellent meanes daily mouing men to the amendment of their finneful life: made by the sayd E. H.
